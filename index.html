Our Story

25 years. Three generations.

The property's been in the family since 1972. We reopened as Kiker's U-Pull-It in 2001 — Hunter was 13, out in the yard breaking down wheels. We've been through the hard times and come back stronger. That history is our edge; nobody around here has it.

Sell us your car and it's not the end of the road. Good parts keep another local family driving, the metal gets recycled the right way, and you get a fair deal for it.
