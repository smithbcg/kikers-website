With a title — or without one

📋 If you have the title

Sign it over — we do the rest

  • ✓Because the vehicle is headed for dismantling, it's processed for a Certificate of Destruction and the title is marked "Cancelled (Junked)" — permanently off your name.
  • ✓Keep your license plate. In Florida the plate stays with you, the seller.
  • ✓As a licensed Florida salvage dealer, we handle the form and the filing.
Source: FLHSMV Form 82363; Fla. Stat. § 319.30.
🔑 No title? You still have options

Florida's derelict-vehicle pathway

  • ✓Florida lets a licensed salvage dealer take a vehicle on a Derelict Motor Vehicle Certificate — generally for vehicles under $1,000 and at least 10 model years old.
  • ✓It's the legal way to handle a no-title junk car — done by the book.
Source: FLHSMV Junk a Vehicle; Form 82137. Full details on our Sell Your Vehicle page.
