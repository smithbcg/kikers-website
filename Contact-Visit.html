Before You Come

Yard rules & safety

A working salvage yard has real hazards. A few simple rules keep everybody safe — and keep your visit quick.

  • 🧰Bring your own tools and gloves. It's self-serve — we don't pull parts for you.
  • 👟Closed-toe shoes required. Heavy steel and sharp metal are everywhere.
  • 🔞16 and up on the yard. For everyone's safety.
  • ⚠️Watch for hazards. Heavy parts, sharp edges, and fluids — and look out for the person next to you.
  • 🏷️Parts are priced at the counter. Pull what you need, pay on your way out.

Good to Know

Visiting Kiker's: FAQ

What are your hours? +
We're open Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 4:30 PM; Saturday, 8 AM to 2 PM, and closed Sunday. Last admission to the yard is 30 minutes before close.
Do I need an appointment? +
No. For parts, just come in during hours. If you're selling a vehicle, call ahead at 850-435-7630 so we can schedule a fast pickup.
Do I need my own tools to pull parts? +
Yes — it's a self-serve yard. Bring your own tools and gloves and pull the parts you need. Parts are priced at the counter.
Is there an age requirement to enter the yard? +
Yes. You must be 16 or older to go out on the yard, and closed-toe shoes are required.
What forms of payment do you accept? +
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and cash.
How do I sell my vehicle to you? +
Call 850-435-7630 or use our Sell Your Vehicle page. We give a fair cash offer, tow it free, handle the paperwork, and pay you — often the same day.
