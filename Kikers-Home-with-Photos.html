Our Story

25 years. Three generations.

Kiker's isn't a faceless online buyer here today and gone tomorrow. We're a Pensacola family that's been through the hard times and come back stronger — and that history is our edge. Nobody around here has it.

When you sell your car to us, it's not the end of the story. The parts go on to keep another family on the road, the metal gets recycled the right way, and the fluids are handled properly. End of life giving life — and you get a fair deal for it.

1972Granddad bought the property. The ground Kiker's still sits on today.
2001Reopened as Kiker's U-Pull-It. Hunter was 13, out in the yard breaking down wheels.
Today3rd generation, family-led. 25 years serving Pensacola — and giving back to the community.
